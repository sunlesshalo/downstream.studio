# Artifacts

Evidence and detailed work products from the DownStream experiment.

## Structure

### analyses/
Deep research and analysis documents.
- Competitor analyses
- Market research
- Technical deep-dives

### experiments/
Results from tests and experiments.
- A/B test results
- Technical experiments
- Marketing experiments

### decisions/
Major decision documents with full context.
- Strategic pivots
- Technology choices
- Business model changes

### conversations/
Excerpts of key dialogue moments.
- Significant philosophical discussions
- Breakthrough conversations
- Important debates

## Naming Convention

`YYYY-MM-DD-descriptive-name.md`

Or for ongoing/updated documents:
`descriptive-name.md` (with date in header)

## Relationship to Other Files

- **Artifacts** are the detailed evidence
- **Chronicle** tells the story and references artifacts
- **Methodology** extracts reusable patterns from artifacts
- **Memory** (STATE.json, CONTEXT.md) is operational, not archival
