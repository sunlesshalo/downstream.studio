Areas for Improvement

CRITICAL

Motion Quality

Final captures (7500px, 7800px, 8045px) showed nearly identical frames. The animation within scenes is too static—closer to still images than "living photographs." Capsules maintains visible fog drift, gentle camera movement, and particle effects throughout.

Fix: Add explicit camera instructions to all video prompts. Implement motion quality gate in production pipeline.

IMPROVEMENT

Opening Hook

Scene 1 shows a dark raven against a dark sky—low contrast, low attention capture. Capsules opens with a RED bird (colorful) MOVING against static grey clouds for maximum scroll-stopping power.

Fix: Opening scenes must have either colorful subject OR visible motion (ideally both). No dark-on-dark openings.

IMPROVEMENT

Bookend Resonance

Opening (raven over city) and closing (shadow puppet theater) have thematic connection but the visual resonance is subtle. This isn't necessarily wrong—subtle callbacks through color, composition, or motion can be more elegant than literal repetition.

Note: The goal is emotional/thematic echo, not didactic visual matching. Subtle references (color callback, compositional parallel, motion echo) are preferable to obvious repetition.
